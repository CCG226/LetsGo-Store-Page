Let's Go Pokedex Tracker Application

A WinUI Desktop Application that helps you keep track of the pokedex progress in Let's Go Pickachu and Let's Go Eevee. It also includes an interactive map of the Kanto Region to make it easier for you to find the next pokemon in your journey to complete the national pokedex.

VISIT STORE PAGE

Includes an interactive map of Kanto! Get Pokemon data by interacting with locations and routes on the map!

Simply tap on a pokemon on the map grid view to get pokedex information!

The interactive map allows you to find out where the pokemon you need are located to help achieve the national pokedex!

Keep track of your Pokedex progess in the MyDex page by checking off the Pokemon you've caught so far. Your Progess is saved locally!
